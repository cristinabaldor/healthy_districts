Healthy Districts


Healthcare outcomes and demographic data by congressional district

These visualizations use publicly available data from the American Community Survey,
the Trust for America's Health, and theunitedstates.io.

Load Data

These two charts plot Median Income and Race against healthcare outcomes. Generally, with higher levels of income and higher proportions of the population who identify as white, negative healthcare outcomes such lack of insurance, smoking, and obesity are less prevalent.



The chart below breaks down the amount of people represented by each seat in the House of Representatives. Populations range from just over 500,000 in each of Rhode Island's two districts, to over 1,000,000 in Montana, which has just one at-large representative.
